6. Key Achievements
  • 5G launch in Addis Ababa (2023) – first in East Africa.
  • Telebirr surpassed 40 million users, revolutionising digital payments.
  • Nationwide fiber backbone extended to 100,000+ km.
  • Partnership with international carriers for submarine cable access.
  • Highest mobile penetration growth in the Horn of Africa.
7. Modernization Projects
  • Network modernization (4G/5G core & RAN upgrade).
  • Tower and fiber infrastructure expansion – 2000 new sites per year.
  • Digital transformation: e‑top up, online self‑care portal.
  • IoT pilot projects for smart agriculture and water management.
8. Impact on Ethiopia
  • Digital economy: enabling e‑commerce, telemedicine, e‑learning.
  • Job creation: direct employment (15,000+) and indirect via agent network (Telebirr).
  • Infrastructure backbone for banks, businesses, and government.
  • Financial inclusion: Telebirr brings banking to unbanked rural areas.
