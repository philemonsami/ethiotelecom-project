1. Overview – Ethio Telecom at a glance

Ethio Telecom (formerly ETC) is the state‑owned telecommunications provider in Ethiopia. Rebranded in 2010, it traces its roots back to 1894 when the first telephone line was introduced in Harar. Today it stands as the backbone of the nation’s digital connectivity.

Mission: Connect Ethiopia through innovative, reliable and affordable telecommunications.
Vision: To be a world‑class digital operator by 2030.
Values: Customer focus, integrity, teamwork, efficiency, and national pride.
2. Services Provided
  • Mobile services: Prepaid/postpaid, 2G/3G/4G/5G, voice, SMS, mobile data packages.
  • Internet services: Broadband, Fiber‑to‑the‑home (FTTH), wireless home internet (WCDMA/4G), dedicated business internet.
  • Telebirr: Digital mobile money – pay bills, top‑up airtime, send/receive money, merchant payments.
  • Enterprise services: Cloud hosting, VPN, machine‑to‑machine (M2M), enterprise connectivity.
  • Fixed telecommunications: Landline telephone, VoIP solutions.

4. Network Coverage
4G/LTE covers all regional capitals and major towns. 5G is live in Addis Ababa, Adama, Bahir Dar, Hawassa, and expanding. Continuous investment in rural connectivity through solar‑powered towers and microwave backhaul. Urban areas enjoy high‑capacity fiber backbones.
