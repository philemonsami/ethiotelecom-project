9. Challenges
  • Network congestion during peak hours in fast‑growing cities.
  • Competition from Safaricom Ethiopia (entered 2021).
  • High infrastructure cost in remote mountainous areas.
  • Power outages affecting towers (mitigated by batteries/generators).
10. Future Plans
  • Expand 5G to 25 cities by 2026.
  • Launch 5G fixed wireless access (FWA) for home broadband.
  • Upgrade core network to full cloud‑native.
  • International partnerships for satellite backhaul (low‑earth orbit).
  • Enhance Telebirr with micro‑loans and savings products.

12. Frequently Asked Questions
QuestionAnswer
How to buy a SIM card?Visit any branch with your kebele ID. Registration is mandatory.
How to register a SIM?Provide fingerprint and photo at service center – it's free.
How to use Telebirr?Dial *127# or use the app. Register with SIM and ID.
How to check balance / data?Dial *800# for mobile; for home internet check via customer portal.
What to do if SIM is lost?Block immediately via 994 and reissue at branch.
